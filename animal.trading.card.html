Fruit Bats!

Did you know, fruit bats has the best eye sight in the bat species? They have dichromatic eyes that contains rod and cone cells in the eyes which allows them to have daylight and night vison

    Scientific Name: mammels

    Average Length:6-12 in (15-30 cm)

    Average Lifespan:15 Years

    Habitat:tropical and subtropical forests

I choose fruit bats because i think they are intresting creatures,and they are very cute. The fruit bats almost sound like a bird when they do a chirping noises.
